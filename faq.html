Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Find what you're looking for below.

We accept all major credit cards (VISA, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and also offer Cash on Delivery (COD) in select areas across India.

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email from us containing a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can use this to track your package's journey to your doorstep.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn and unwashed items with all original tags still attached. Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for returns. For more details, please visit our Shipping & Returns page.

We process orders very quickly to ensure you get your items as soon as possible. If you need to make a change or cancel, please contact us immediately. If the order has already been shipped, you will need to follow our standard return process once you receive it.

Currently, Urban Threads only ships within India. We are actively working on expanding our shipping network to bring our styles to customers worldwide. Stay tuned for updates!
