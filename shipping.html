Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about our shipping and returns process.

Shipping Policy

Order Processing Times

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Domestic Shipping Rates and Estimates

We offer flat rate shipping across India. Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): ₹50
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days): ₹100
  • Free standard shipping on orders over ₹1000.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and may vary due to factors outside our control.

How do I check the status of my order?

When your order has shipped, you will receive an email notification from us which will include a tracking number you can use to check its status. Please allow 48 hours for the tracking information to become available.

Returns & Exchanges Policy

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns or exchanges on unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached within 30 days of the delivery date.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be in new condition, with all original tags and packaging intact.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged.
  • We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet these criteria.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

To start a return or exchange, please visit our online return portal or contact our customer support team at support@urbanthreads.com with your order number and the reason for your return. We will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item(s), we will process your refund to the original method of payment within 5-7 business days. You will be notified via email once your refund has been processed.
