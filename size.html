Size Guide

Find your perfect fit. All measurements are in inches.

How To Measure

To ensure the best fit, compare your measurements with the charts below. For best results, measure yourself with a tape measure over your undergarments.

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, the narrowest part of your torso.
  • Hips: Stand with your feet together and measure around the fullest part of your hips.
